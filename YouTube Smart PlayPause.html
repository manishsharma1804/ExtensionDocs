How It Works

YouTube Auto Pause is designed to enhance your browsing experience by intelligently managing video playback:

  • Automatically pauses videos when you switch tabs
  • Pauses when you switch to another window
  • Optionally resumes playback when you return
  • Simple toggle to enable/disable the extension

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's what you need to know:

  • No personal data collection
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No external server communication
  • All preferences stored locally

Required Permissions

  • tabs: To detect tab switching and identify YouTube tabs
  • host_permissions for YouTube: To control video playback
  • storage: To save your preferences locally

Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't the extension work on embedded YouTube videos? +
The extension is designed to work specifically with youtube.com domain. Embedded videos on other websites require different permissions and handling.
How do I temporarily disable the extension? +
Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar and use the main toggle button to enable/disable the extension. Your preferences will be saved.
Does this work with other video platforms? +
Currently, the extension is designed specifically for YouTube. Support for other platforms may be added in future updates.
Will this slow down my browser? +
No, the extension is designed to be lightweight and efficient. It only runs when you're on YouTube and uses minimal resources.
